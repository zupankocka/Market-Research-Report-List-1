<p><p>The bulk desiccants market comprises various types that effectively control moisture. Clay desiccants are natural materials that absorb water vapor and are eco-friendly. Silica gel, a widely used option, offers high absorption capacity and can be reused after drying. Calcium oxide, known for its strong hygroscopic properties, rapidly absorbs moisture but is typically less common in bulk applications. Calcium sulfate, often used in situations requiring moderate moisture control, is stable and less reactive, making it suitable for various industrial uses.</p></p>
