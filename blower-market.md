<p><p>Blower markets are categorized into low pressure, medium voltage, and high pressure based on the pressure capabilities and applications of the blowers. Low pressure blowers operate typically under 2 psi and are used for tasks like aeration in wastewater treatment. Medium voltage blowers, ranging from 2 to 15 psi, serve industrial processes requiring moderate pressure. High pressure blowers exceed 15 psi and are essential in applications such as pneumatic conveying and fluid transfer, where significant pressure is necessary for efficiency.</p></p>
