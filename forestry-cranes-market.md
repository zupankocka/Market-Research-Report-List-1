<p><p>The forestry cranes market comprises three main types: A-Line, L-Line, and Z-Line cranes. A-Line cranes are characterized by their articulated arms, providing excellent maneuverability for lifting and placing logs in various terrains. L-Line cranes feature a linear design, ideal for long reach applications, facilitating efficient loading and unloading of timber. Z-Line cranes have a zigzag arm configuration, offering enhanced stability and reaching capabilities in dense forestry environments. Each type is designed to meet specific operational needs in timber handling and forestry management.</p></p>
