<p><strong>The Universal Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Velocity Joint</li><li>Cross-axis Universal Joint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The universal joint market encompasses various types, primarily the Constant Velocity Joint (CV Joint) and the Cross-axis Universal Joint. CV joints are designed to maintain a constant output speed, ensuring smooth power transmission even at varying angles, commonly used in front-wheel-drive vehicles. In contrast, Cross-axis Universal Joints allow for greater angular movement and are typically employed in applications requiring flexibility and adaptability, such as in drive shafts of heavy machinery. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle performance and drivetrain efficiency.</p></p>
