<p><ul><li>Homopolymer Type</li><li>Copolymer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PP (Polypropylene) Compounds market is categorized into Homopolymer and Copolymer types. Homopolymer PP compounds are made from a single monomer, offering high strength, stiffness, and chemical resistance, making them ideal for rigid applications like automotive parts and packaging. Copolymer PP compounds, derived from two monomers, provide enhanced impact resistance, flexibility, and toughness, suitable for durable goods, containers, and industrial components. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing performance, cost, and application-specific requirements.</p></p>
