<p><p>The Polyurethane Foam Additives market can be categorized into two main types: hard and soft foam. Hard foam additives are designed to enhance structural strength, thermal insulation, and rigidity, making them suitable for applications like insulation panels and automotive parts. In contrast, soft foam additives focus on flexibility, comfort, and cushioning properties, commonly used in mattresses, upholstery, and packaging. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, catering to specific performance requirements and applications.</p></p>
