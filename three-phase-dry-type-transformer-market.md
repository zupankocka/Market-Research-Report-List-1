<p><strong>The Three Phase Dry Type Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Winding Transformer</li><li>Three Winding Transformer</li><li>Self-Couple Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Three Phase Dry Type Transformer market encompasses three main types: </p><p>1. **Double Winding Transformer**: This transformer features two windings, primary and secondary, allowing efficient voltage transformation and isolation between circuits.</p><p>2. **Three Winding Transformer**: This type includes an additional tertiary winding, enabling complex power distribution and improved load management across multiple systems.</p><p>3. **Self-Coupled Transformer**: With windings that tap into the same coil, this transformer offers voltage regulation and is often used for specific applications requiring minimal space and cost efficiency. </p><p>These variations cater to different industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
