<p><p>The HVAC heater control valve market includes various types based on functionality and application. Two-way valves regulate flow in one direction, essential for basic heating systems. Three-way valves can mix or divert flow, optimizing temperature control in larger systems. Four-way valves further enhance system flexibility by allowing both heating and cooling. Five-way valves offer advanced control for complex multi-zone systems, while "others" encompass unique configurations tailored to specific applications. Each type plays a crucial role in improving HVAC efficiency and performance.</p></p>
