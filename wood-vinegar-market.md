<p><strong>The Wood Vinegar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slow Pyrolysis</li><li>Fast Pyrolysis</li><li>Intermediate Pyrolysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood vinegar market is categorized into three types based on the pyrolysis process: slow pyrolysis, fast pyrolysis, and intermediate pyrolysis. Slow pyrolysis operates at low temperatures and longer residence times, yielding higher quality wood vinegar with enhanced properties. Fast pyrolysis, on the other hand, occurs at higher temperatures, producing wood vinegar rapidly but often with lower quality. Intermediate pyrolysis balances the two, offering a compromise between yield and quality, catering to varying industrial and agricultural applications.</p></p>
