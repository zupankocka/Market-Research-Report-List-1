<p><p>The 2-Phase Switched Reluctance Motor (SRM) is an electric motor that operates on the principle of reluctance torque. Unlike traditional motors, it does not rely on permanent magnets or windings on the rotor. Instead, it has a simple and robust construction, comprising a stator with winding and a rotor that distributes magnetic reluctance. This design yields high efficiency, low maintenance, and durability, making it suitable for various applications including automotive, industrial drives, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The 2-Phase Switched Reluctance Motor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.1% during the forecast period. Furthermore, the trend towards automation in industrial processes and the need for precise control in various applications are contributing factors. Emerging technologies, such as enhanced control algorithms and integration with smart systems, are also shaping the market landscape.
