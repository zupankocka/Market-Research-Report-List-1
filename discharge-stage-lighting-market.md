<p><p>The discharge stage lighting market comprises three main types of lamps: Low Pressure Gas Discharge Lamps, High Pressure Discharge Lamps, and Super High Pressure Gas Discharge Lamps. Low Pressure Gas Discharge Lamps produce light using low-energy gas, ideal for broad illumination. High Pressure Discharge Lamps generate brighter, more intense light suitable for large venues. Super High Pressure Gas Discharge Lamps emit extremely bright light, often used for specialized applications like film production or large-scale outdoor events, offering exceptional color rendering and efficiency.</p></p>
