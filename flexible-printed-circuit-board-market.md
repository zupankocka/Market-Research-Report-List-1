<p><p>The Flexible Printed Circuit Board (FPCB) market is primarily categorized into single-sided and double-sided FPCBs. Single-sided FPCBs consist of a flexible substrate with conductive pathways printed on one side, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for basic applications. Double-sided FPCBs have conductive layers on both sides, allowing for more complex circuit designs and enhanced functionality, suitable for advanced electronics. Both types cater to various industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices, reflecting the growing demand for compact and lightweight solutions.</p></p>
