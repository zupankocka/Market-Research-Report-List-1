<p><ul><li>High Pressure</li><li>Medium Pressure</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal air blowers are categorized into high, medium, and low pressure based on their discharge pressure and application. High-pressure blowers, typically exceeding 15 psi, are used in applications requiring forceful airflow, such as pneumatic conveying and combustion air supplies. Medium-pressure blowers, ranging from 5 to 15 psi, suit various industrial processes. Low-pressure blowers operate below 5 psi and are ideal for venting, cooling, and aeration in wastewater treatment. Each type serves distinct operational needs in diverse industries.</p></p>
