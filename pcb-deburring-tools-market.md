<p><p>The PCB deburring tools market is categorized into two main types: abrasive and non-abrasive deburring tools. Abrasive deburring tools use materials like sandpaper and grinding wheels to remove burrs, effectively smoothing edges and enhancing surface finish. In contrast, non-abrasive deburring tools include methods such as ultrasonic cleaning, air jets, and chemical treatments, which eliminate burrs without material removal. Both types are essential for achieving precision and reliability in PCB manufacturing, ensuring optimal performance in electronic applications.</p></p>
