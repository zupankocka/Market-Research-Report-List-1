<p><ul><li>Torque Converter Friction Plate</li><li>Torque Converter Sprag Clutche</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lockup torque converter market includes two primary types: torque converter friction plates and torque converter sprag clutches. Torque converter friction plates enhance the connection between the engine and transmission, improving efficiency and reducing slippage during operation. In contrast, torque converter sprag clutches allow for one-way rotation, ensuring smooth engagement and disengagement of the torque converter. Both components play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle performance, fuel efficiency, and driving comfort in automatic transmission systems.</p></p>
