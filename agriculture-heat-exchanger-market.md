<p><p>The Agriculture Heat Exchanger Market is primarily categorized into Plate and Tubular heat exchangers. Plate heat exchangers consist of thin plates arranged in a frame, offering high surface area for efficient heat transfer and compact design, making them suitable for processing agricultural products. Tubular heat exchangers, characterized by their cylindrical tubes, provide robust performance and can handle high-pressure applications, making them ideal for larger-scale operations. Both types are essential for optimizing temperature control in various agricultural processes.</p></p>
