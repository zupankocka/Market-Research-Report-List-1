<p><p>The online ticketing system market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise systems. Cloud-based online ticketing systems operate through the internet, allowing for easy access, scalability, and lower upfront costs, making them ideal for businesses that prefer flexibility and remote management. In contrast, on-premise online ticketing systems are installed locally on a business's own servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring higher initial investments and maintenance. Both models cater to varying operational needs and user preferences.</p></p>
