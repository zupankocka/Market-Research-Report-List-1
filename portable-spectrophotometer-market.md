<p><strong>The Portable Spectrophotometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Beam Type</li><li>Double Beam Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable spectrophotometer market comprises two main types: single beam and double beam. Single beam spectrophotometers analyze light intensity variations in a single path, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for basic applications. In contrast, double beam spectrophotometers use two light paths—one for the sample and another for a reference—allowing for improved accuracy and stability by compensating for light source fluctuations. Both types cater to diverse industries, including environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and food quality testing.</p></p>
