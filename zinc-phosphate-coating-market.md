<p><p>Zinc phosphate coating is a chemical conversion coating applied to metal surfaces, primarily steel and iron, to enhance corrosion resistance, improve paint adhesion, and provide a base for lubrication. The coating process involves immersing the metal in a solution containing zinc phosphate, which reacts with the surface to form a crystalline layer.</p><p>The Zinc Phosphate Coating Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand from end-use industries and advancements in coating technologies.
