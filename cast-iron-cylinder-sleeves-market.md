<p><p>The cast iron cylinder sleeves market features two primary types: dry liners and wet liners. Dry liners are installed without any cooling jacket and rely on the engine block for heat dissipation, offering durability and easy maintenance. Conversely, wet liners are part of the cooling system, surrounded by coolant, which enhances thermal management and reduces overheating risks. Both types are essential for engine performance, catering to varying requirements in automotive and industrial applications, thus driving their market demand.</p></p>
