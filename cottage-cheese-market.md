Processed cheese incorporates emulsifiers and other ingredients for enhanced meltability and shelf-life, often used in culinary applications. Cheese food includes products that contain lower cheese content combined with additional dairy ingredients, offering a budget-friendly option with a milder taste, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
