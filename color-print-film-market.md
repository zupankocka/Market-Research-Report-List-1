<p><strong>The Color Print Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Film</li><li>Water-Based Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The color print film market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based films. Solvent-based films utilize organic solvents, providing vibrant color outputs and durability, making them suitable for outdoor applications. In contrast, water-based films use water as a primary solvent, offering a more environmentally friendly option with reduced VOC emissions. While water-based films may have limitations in color vibrancy compared to solvent-based, they are favored for indoor use, packaging, and applications where sustainability is a priority.</p></p>
