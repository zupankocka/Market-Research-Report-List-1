<p><p>Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) devices are medical equipment primarily used to treat obstructive sleep apnea (OSA), a condition characterized by repeated interruptions of breathing during sleep. Ongoing innovations in device design, including portable and more user-friendly options, are further driving demand.
