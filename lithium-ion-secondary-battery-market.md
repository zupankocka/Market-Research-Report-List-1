<p><p>The lithium-ion secondary battery market is categorized into three main types: cylindrical, prismatic, and polymer batteries. Cylindrical batteries are known for their robust design and high energy density, commonly used in power tools and electric vehicles. Prismatic batteries feature a flat, rectangular shape, allowing for efficient space utilization in applications like smartphones and laptops. Polymer batteries, utilizing a solid or gel-like electrolyte, offer flexibility in design and are often employed in compact electronic devices. Each type serves distinct applications based on size, energy requirements, and performance characteristics.</p></p>
