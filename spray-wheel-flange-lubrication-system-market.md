<p><p>The Spray Wheel Flange Lubrication System market is categorized into single-line and dual-line systems. Single-line systems deliver lubricant through a single pipeline, allowing for straightforward installation and maintenance, making them suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, dual-line systems utilize two pipelines, enabling simultaneous lubrication to multiple points, which is ideal for larger or more complex machinery. Both types enhance operational efficiency and reduce wear and tear on equipment by ensuring consistent lubrication at critical contact points.</p></p>
