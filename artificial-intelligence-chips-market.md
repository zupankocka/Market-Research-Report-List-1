<p><p>The artificial intelligence chip market includes several key types: GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) excel in handling parallel tasks, making them ideal for deep learning applications. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) are custom-designed for specific tasks, providing high efficiency and performance for targeted AI applications. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) offer flexibility with the ability to be reconfigured for various tasks, balancing performance and adaptability. CPUs (Central Processing Units) serve general-purpose computing needs but are often less efficient for specialized AI workloads compared to the other chip types.</p></p>
