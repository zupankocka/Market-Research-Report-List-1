<p><p>Low Dropout (LDO) regulators are essential in power management, categorized into two main types: adjustable and fixed. Adjustable LDOs allow users to set the output voltage to their specific requirements by changing external resistors, offering flexibility for various applications. Fixed LDOs, on the other hand, provide a predetermined output voltage, simplifying design and usage. Both types cater to different needs, balancing factors such as cost, space, and performance in electronic devices and systems across multiple industries.</p></p>
