<p><p>Semiconductor surface treatment furnaces are crucial in the manufacturing process, with two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal furnaces offer enhanced thermal uniformity and are ideal for batch processing of wafers, making them suitable for larger production volumes. In contrast, vertical furnaces are characterized by a smaller footprint and efficient space utilization, making them effective for high-throughput applications. Both types play a vital role in cleaning, oxidizing, and preparing semiconductor surfaces for subsequent fabrication steps, contributing to the overall quality of electronic components.</p></p>
