<p><p>Polyacrylamide for the oilfield market is primarily categorized into two types: emulsion and powder. Emulsion polyacrylamide is used for enhanced oil recovery, offering improved stability and easier application in various reservoir conditions. It effectively reduces fluid viscosity and enhances displacement efficiency. On the other hand, powder polyacrylamide is typically used in drilling fluids and completion fluids, providing viscosity control and aiding in fluid loss control. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing oil extraction processes and improving overall efficiency.</p></p>
