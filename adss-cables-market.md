<p><ul><li>ADSS Flextube</li><li>ADSS Loose Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ADSS cables, or All Dielectric Self-Supporting cables, are designed for overhead installations without the need for additional support. The ADSS Flextube type incorporates multiple tightly packed fibers in a flexible tube, allowing for improved performance in challenging environments where movement or vibration may occur. In contrast, the ADSS Loose Tube design features fibers housed in a loose structure, providing extra protection against thermal expansion and mechanical stress, making it ideal for longer spans and varied climatic conditions.</p></p>
