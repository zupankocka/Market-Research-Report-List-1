<p><strong>The Volumetric Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Volume Display</li><li>Solid-Volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Volumetric displays create 3D images by illuminating points in a physical space, offering true depth perception. **Swept-Volume Displays** use a rapidly moving screen or surface to project images, creating the illusion of a 3D object as the screen sweeps through space. **Solid-Volume Displays** generate images within a static, transparent medium, such as a crystal or gas, using lasers or light to form 3D visuals. Both types cater to applications like medical imaging, gaming, and design, providing immersive, interactive experiences.</p></p>
