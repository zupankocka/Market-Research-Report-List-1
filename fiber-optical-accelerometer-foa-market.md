<p><strong>The Fiber Optical Accelerometer (FOA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Axis</li><li>Single-Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Optical Accelerometers (FOA) utilize light fibers to measure acceleration by detecting changes in light properties caused by motion. The market is segmented into two main types: Multi-Axis and Single-Axis accelerometers. Multi-Axis FOAs can simultaneously measure acceleration across multiple directions, making them suitable for complex applications like robotics and aerospace. In contrast, Single-Axis FOAs focus on one direction, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for applications requiring less intricate motion tracking, such as consumer electronics and automotive systems.</p></p>
