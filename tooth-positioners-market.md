<p><strong>The Tooth Positioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-extraction Positioners</li><li>Extraction Positioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tooth Positioners market consists of two main types: non-extraction positioners and extraction positioners. Non-extraction positioners are designed to adjust tooth alignment without the removal of any teeth, making them ideal for patients who have a full set of dentition. Conversely, extraction positioners are utilized when specific teeth are removed, helping to realign and correctly position the remaining teeth for optimal occlusion and aesthetics. Both types play vital roles in orthodontic treatment by facilitating proper dental alignment.</p></p>
