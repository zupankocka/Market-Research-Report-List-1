<p><strong>The Organic Pigments for Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Azo Pigments</li><li>Non-azo Pigments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic pigments market for inks comprises primarily two categories: azo pigments and non-azo pigments. Azo pigments, characterized by their vibrant colors and excellent lightfastness, are widely used in various inks due to their affordability and versatility. Non-azo pigments, including phthalocyanine and quinacridone types, offer superior stability and resistance to chemicals and UV light, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Both pigment types play crucial roles in determining the properties, color strength, and durability of ink products.</p></p>
