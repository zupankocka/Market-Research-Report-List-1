<p><p>The batch coding machines market is primarily divided into two types: contact coding and non-contact coding. Contact coding machines directly imprint information onto the surface of products using methods like stamp or pad printing, offering precision for various materials. In contrast, non-contact coding machines utilize technologies such as inkjet or laser printing, allowing for marking without physical contact, which helps to prevent damage to delicate items. Both types are essential for industries requiring accurate product identification and information.</p></p>
