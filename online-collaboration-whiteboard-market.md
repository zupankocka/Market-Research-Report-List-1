<p><p>The online collaboration whiteboard market is primarily categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise whiteboards are installed directly on a company’s servers, allowing for greater control over data security and customization, but requiring significant IT infrastructure. In contrast, cloud-based whiteboards operate over the internet, offering accessibility from any location and facilitating real-time collaboration among remote teams. This type typically involves subscription-based pricing and automatic updates, making it convenient for dynamic work environments.</p></p>
