<p><strong>The Gene Sequencer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plate Electrophoresis</li><li>Capillary Electrophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gene sequencer market is primarily divided into two types: plate electrophoresis and capillary electrophoresis. Plate electrophoresis utilizes a flat substrate for the separation of DNA fragments, allowing for high-throughput analysis suitable for large-scale projects. In contrast, capillary electrophoresis employs thin capillaries filled with a gel or polymer to achieve precise separation of nucleic acids. This method is known for its efficiency, speed, and sensitivity, making it ideal for detailed sequencing applications. Both techniques are crucial for advancements in genomics and biotechnology.</p></p>
