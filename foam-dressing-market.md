<p><p>The foam dressing market is categorized into two main types: adhesive foam dressings and non-adhesive foam dressings. Adhesive foam dressings feature a sticky backing that allows direct application to the wound site, providing a secure hold and promoting healing. In contrast, non-adhesive foam dressings are designed to absorb exudate while typically requiring an additional securing method, such as tape or wraps. Both types are used to manage wounds, enhance comfort, and maintain a moist healing environment.</p></p>
