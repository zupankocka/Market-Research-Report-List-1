<p><p>Soft magnetic powder refers to finely granulated materials with high magnetic permeability and low coercivity, making them ideal for applications requiring efficient magnetization and demagnetization. These powders are widely used in industries such as electronics, automotive, energy, and telecommunications for manufacturing components like inductors, transformers, motors, and sensors. Common materials include iron, iron-silicon, iron-nickel, and iron-cobalt alloys, often coated with insulating layers to reduce eddy current losses.</p><p>The Soft Magnetic Powder Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient devices, advancements in electric vehicle (EV) technology, and the rise of renewable energy systems.
