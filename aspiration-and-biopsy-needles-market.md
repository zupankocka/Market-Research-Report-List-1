<p><p>The aspiration and biopsy needles market comprises two main types: fine-needle aspiration (FNA) needles and biopsy needles. Fine-needle aspiration involves using a thin needle to extract cellular material from tissues for diagnostic purposes, primarily in oncology and cytology. Biopsy needles, on the other hand, are designed to remove larger tissue samples for histopathological examination. Both types are essential for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning, catering to various medical fields such as oncology, pathology, and interventional radiology.</p></p>
