<p><strong>The Indirect Water Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Coiled Type</li><li>External Submerged Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Indirect water heaters utilize an external heat source to warm water, with specific types catering to different needs. The internal coiled type features a coil within the tank where hot water circulates, efficiently heating the stored water. This design is ideal for smaller spaces. Conversely, the external submerged type has a heat exchanger submerged in the tank, allowing for larger capacities and better heat distribution, making it suitable for larger residential or commercial requirements. Both types ensure effective water heating through indirect methods.</p></p>
