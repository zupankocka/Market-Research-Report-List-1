<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Host Cell Protein Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCR-based Assays</li><li>ELISA-based Assays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Host Cell Protein Testing Market is primarily categorized into PCR-based assays and ELISA-based assays. PCR-based assays leverage polymerase chain reaction techniques to detect and quantify host cell proteins with high sensitivity and specificity, making them crucial for ensuring the purity of biopharmaceutical products. In contrast, ELISA-based assays utilize enzyme-linked immunosorbent techniques to identify and measure host cell proteins through antigen-antibody interactions, offering a reliable and easier-to-interpret approach. Both methods are essential for regulatory compliance and product safety in biopharmaceutical development.</p></p>
